KUBANG KERIAN

Kubang Kerian is a parliamentary seat in Kota Bharu District, Kelantan, Malaysia. Here is the Universiti Sains Malaysia (USM) Health Campus.USM Health Campus is another name for this campus. The School of Medical Sciences, the School of Dentistry, and the School of Health Sciences are all located on the site. On site, there is a teaching hospital with 760 beds. The Hospital Universiti Sains Malaysia (HUSM) is located in Malaysia.

Depending on traffic, Kubang Kerian is about a 10-15 minutes journey on route 8 from Kota Bharu, the capital city. Kubang Kerian is around 15-20 minutes from Pengkalan Chepa's airport. If commuters prefer to take a cab from the airport to Kubang Kerian, an RM30 fee will be imposed. If you take a taxi between 12 a.m. and 6 a.m., you will be charged an additional 50 percent per night service charge of RM45.

Kubang Kerian has emerged as a new economic centre in Kelantan. Since 1983, the establishment of the USM Health Campus in Kubang Kerian has attracted additional industries, which has helped to enhance the local economy. Kubang Kerian now has a fresh market, daily night markets, a Friday night market, a Monday night market, several fresh chicken slaughter outlets, a few photoshops, two pharmacies, two bookshops, one shoe shop, many beauty shops, and numerous eateries such as bakeries, KFC, Domino's, Pizza Hut, Subway, D'Klasik, One Stop Kopitiam, and Prima House.
