Systematic Ultrasound for Critical Care and
Emergency Scenarios (SUCCES) Course

SUCCES is a fundamental ultrasound course that seeks to give students the abilities needed to carry out and interpret
certain ultrasonographic examinations depending on clinical circumstances. The ultrasonographic examination, which
is sometimes multi-systemic and serves as an extension of a critically ill patient's clinical examination, is essential for
making a quick and correct clinical judgement. The training is a two-day programme that includes small-group
instruction on live models to give participants plenty of chance for additional hands-on practise.

The course is intended for professionals who work in the field of emergency and critical care, including surgeons,
anesthesiologists, intensivists, emergency physicians, and physicians who specialise in acute care.


Organizer: Society of Critical & Emergency Ultrasound Malaysia

E-mail: succes_malaysia@yahoo.com

Date: 9th August 2023

Venue: Hospital Universiti Sains Malaysia (HUSM), Kubang Kerian, Kelantan
